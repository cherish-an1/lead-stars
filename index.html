Hepatology

Our hepatology unit focuses on the diagnosis, treatment and management of liver and related conditions. The unit typically offers a range of services, including:. Consultations for patients with liver diseases, including viral hepatitis, cirrhosis, and liver cancer. Medical treatments such as antiviral therapy for hepatitis, medications to reduce inflammation and prevent liver damage, and transplantation.

All Departments

Dental

We offer Dental services which include regular checkups and cleanings, fillings and extractions, root canals, crowns and bridges, implants, orthodontic treatment, and periodontal therapy. our dentists may also offer cosmetic dental procedures such as teeth whitening, veneers, and bonding. Dental services may also include the use of diagnostic tools such as X-rays, digital impressions, and 3D scans to diagnose and plan treatment.

All Departments

Ophthalmology

Our ophthalmology unit is a specialized medical diagnosis and treatment for eye disorders and diseases. We have Ophthalmologists, who are specialized in the diagnosis and treatment of eye conditions. We provide services such as eye exams, prescriptions for glasses or contact lenses, and treatment for conditions such as glaucoma, cataracts, and age-related macular degeneration. We also perform surgical procedures, such as cataract removal, to restore or improve vision.

All Departments

Otology

We have a unit that deals with the diagnosis and treatment of ear disorders. This includes conditions such as hearing loss, tinnitus, balance disorders, and ear infections. We have medical specialists who are trained to diagnose and treat ear-related conditions. They may also perform surgical procedures to repair or remove damaged structures in the ear.
